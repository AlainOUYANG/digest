## 因果推断与营销科学

- **[Higher-order Spillover Effects Under Partial Interference](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.12855)** — Qixiang Xu, Laura Forastiere
  探讨网络交互中高阶溢出效应，指出邻域干扰假设过于严格，提出新方法。
- **[Significance-First Splitting: Aligning Treatment Heterogeneity Detection with Honest Estimation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.03999)** — Pantelis Z. Hadjipantelis, Weng Man Chiang, Karthik Nagesh
  提出显著性优先分裂方法，在检测处理效应异质性时同时保证有效推断和估计准确性。
- **[Orthogonal Integrated Conditional Moment Tests for Treatment Effect Heterogeneity](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.12622)** — Haokun Lu, Xiaojun Song
  提出非参数检验方法，通过正交得分降低估计误差影响，检验协变量子集定义亚组的处理效应异质性。
- **[Modification and extension of the Bayesian clinical trial design using external data for single-arm and hybrid-controlled trials](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.12521)** — Wataru Murasaki, Tomohiro Ohigashi, Ryota Ishii, Kazushi Maruo, Masahiko Gosho
  改进贝叶斯单臂和混合对照试验设计，解决外部数据导致I类错误率失控问题。
- **[Factorial clinical trials in the presence and absence of plausible statistical interactions between treatment factors. A historical review of the methodological literature](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.12991)** — Rebecca E. A. Walwyn, Pritaporn Kingkaew, Alan A. Montgomery, Alexandra Wright-Hughes, Steven G. Gilmour
  综述析因试验方法学历史，分析有无交互作用时的设计差异和误解。
- **[Diagrams-to-Dynamics (D2D): Exploring Causal Loop Diagram Leverage Points under Uncertainty](https://arxiv.org/abs/2508.05659)** — Jeroen F. Uleman, Loes Crielaard, Leonie K. Elsenburg, Guido A. Veldhuis, Naja Hulvej Rod, Rick Quax, V\'itor V. Vasconcelos
  提出将因果循环图转化为系统动力学模型的方法，支持动态分析和干预策略探索。
